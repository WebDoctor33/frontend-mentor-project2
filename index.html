Simple Omellete Recipe

Here is a simple recipe for a delicious omelette. This recipe is for a single serving omelette, but you can easily adjust the ingredients to make more servings. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup of diced vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined.
  2. Stir in the cheese, vegetables, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the edges start to set.
  5. Using a spatula, gently lift the edges of the omelette and tilt the skillet to allow the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the omelette is set and the bottom is golden brown.
  7. Using the spatula, fold the omelette in half and slide it onto a plate.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

Feel free to customize this recipe with your favorite ingredients, such as ham, bacon, spinach, or tomatoes. The possibilities are endless!
